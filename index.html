Hideous Uniforms

Seriously hard to look at

The National Football League is a $13 billion industry. That might be difficult to understand since some of the uniforms in the league appear to have been designed children. Some of the color pallettes appear to be a random selection of colors from a crayon box. The worst offenders are the "Color Rush" uniforms that the NFL has debuted on Thursday Night Football.

For your viewing displeasure, listed below are some of the worst perpetrators.

  • Tampa Bay Buccaneers and LA Rams
  • New York Jets and Buffalo Bills
  • San Diego Chargers and Denver Broncos
  • Jacksonville Jaguars and Tennessee Titans
